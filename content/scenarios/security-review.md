Roleplay setup:
- A team wants to adopt a new SaaS tool; the AI plays the requesting team lead
- Goal: ask about data handled, auth method, admin controls; give a conditional approval
> Thanks for bringing this tool to us for review.
> Before we approve it, I have a few security questions.
> Could you tell me what kind of data this tool will handle?
